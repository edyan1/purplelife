Purple Life

Objective

The objective of this game is quite simple. You must destroy the target with the given weapons.

Once you have selected your weapon of choice from the left fan-out menu "W" you can adjust its direction with the right fan-out menu labeled "D" and then place it on the field within the green area.

When the play button is pressed the game will begin and the weaopons will launch in the direction that was chosen.
If the targets are successfully destroyed then you have the option of advancing to the the next level.


Extras

If a user is signed in than he/she can check their progress and create custom levels from existing templetes or create their own.
